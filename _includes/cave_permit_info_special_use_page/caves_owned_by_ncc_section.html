CAVES OWNED BY THE NORTHEASTERN CAVE CONSERVANCY (NCC)

General NCC Checklist (for all NCC caves):
  • Contact the group use coordinators to make a reservation at specialuse@necaveconservancy.org
  • A certificate of additionally insured naming the Northeastern Cave Conservancy Inc as additionally insured should be emailed as a PDF to specialuse@necaveconservancy.org or mailed to Northeastern Cave Conservancy, Inc., PO Box 254, Schoharie NY 12157.
  • Note: All certificates of additionally insured naming the NCC inc must verify a minimum of $1,000,000 per occurrence liability coverage.
  • Provide a Notification of Risk for the entire party. If the group intends to visit more than one NCC preserve, there should be one form provided for each preserve. The signature must be original and the form should be mailed by US Postal Service to the NCC.
  • No more than 15 people total, including leaders, in a group. (Total of 14 for scout groups including at least 2 leaders)
  • Scout groups must follow the National Scout rules for caving and complete the BSA NCC-NSS form. For BSA rules click here.
  • For all caves only four (4) special use slots are available per cave per day. In July and August we permit groups to reserve only 2 slots per day for any given cave.
  • Once paperwork is complete, the group use coordinator will provide the group with a window permit to be displayed in the car window while parked at the cave.
  • Camping is not allowed on any NCC property.
  • Please note that there are no bathroom facilities at NCC preserves. Please plan ahead. The NCC supports the principles of Leave No Trace.
